Hair Loss Fact

  • Up to 50% of men and 40% of women will experience hair loss (alopecia) in their lifetime.
  • The most common cause is genetic (androgenic alopecia), due to hormones such as Dihydrotestosterone (DHT).
  • Less common causes include auto-immune conditions, stress, tight hairstyles and many more

Hair Transplant Effects

  • Hair transplants are done under local anesthetic, making them painless
  • Older style hair transplants can leave scars or have unnatural results.
  • DHI Hair Transplants can achieve the highest graft density in the industry, without strips or scars.
  • DHI Hair transplants are permanent.
